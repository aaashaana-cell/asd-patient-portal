Patient Questionnaires

Please complete the form(s) requested by your doctor. Each questionnaire takes 10 to 20 minutes.

Before you begin

Your clinician has sent you these questionnaires as part of an autism assessment. Please read through the following before you start.

  • These questionnaires are screening tools, not a diagnostic assessment. They help your clinician understand your experiences before and during the clinical interview.
  • A structured clinical interview, conducted separately, is where a diagnostic conclusion can be reached. The questionnaires you are completing today are part of that conversation and will inform it.
  • A diagnostic conclusion requires both stages: the information you provide here, and the clinical interview with your clinician. Neither alone is sufficient.
  • Some questions ask about childhood experiences as well as your current life. Answer as best you can. Not remembering everything clearly is completely normal, and rough or uncertain answers are still useful.
A note before you start. Some of these forms are long, and a few questions may feel repetitive or hard to answer. This is intentional. The overlap helps your clinician build a fuller picture across different areas of your life.

Please answer as honestly as you can. If a question is unclear, doesn't apply, or you simply don't know the answer, it's fine to leave it blank or skip past it. A partially completed form is still useful. Please submit whatever you can.

Privacy & transparency notice

Privacy & Transparency Notice

Your data stays on your device

All responses you enter are processed entirely within your browser. Nothing is transmitted to any server while you are completing a questionnaire. Your answers are only sent to your clinician when you actively choose to download the results file and share it yourself.

Encrypted results file

When you download your results, they are encrypted before leaving your device. The results file contains your responses and basic session information. Only your clinician can open it.

No AI analysis of your data

Your responses are not analysed, processed, or seen by any artificial intelligence system. The questionnaires themselves are established clinical instruments. AI tools were used to code and format them into this digital interface, but the content of what you submit is handled exclusively by your clinician.

Clinical confidentiality

Once received by your clinician, your results are subject to standard clinical confidentiality obligations. They form part of your clinical assessment and are not shared with third parties without your consent.

Questions

If you have any questions about how your information is used, please speak to your clinician directly.
